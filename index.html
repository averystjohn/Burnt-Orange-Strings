Moody Groove Concert at Moody CMB Studio 6B

November 13th @ 1:00 PM

Moody Grove Concert live at the Moody CMB Studio 6B on November 13th from 1:00-2:00 PM! Come see Burnt Orange String's punk rock music as well as performance from a special guest. Free admission and hot cocoa, come check us out on campus in between classes!

About

Burnt Orange Strings is a punk rock band of UT students from the class RTF 347C: Intro to the Music Business. Our performers are Nash Brayden, Emerson Witherspoon, Brooke Bickham, Sophia Brinkman, Paul Snow, Jet Beck, and Presley McGurl. We're hoping to see fellow UT students come see us at the Moody Groove concert!
